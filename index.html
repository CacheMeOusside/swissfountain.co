Story

On a summer holiday in Switzerland, I was captivated by the Swiss unique relationship with water. From the rivers winding through cities to the public fountains scattered across both urban and rural areas, water seemed to be a vital and integral part of daily life. In Zurich, I joined locals as they swam in rivers and ran along lakes that bordered farmland. In Lugano, I swam in alpine lakes, wakeboarded on pristine waters, and drank from the refreshing fountains that dotted the city. Inspired by this, I'm seizing the opportunity to bring the exclusive and revitalizing experience of Swiss water to discerning customers worldwide. - Dax
